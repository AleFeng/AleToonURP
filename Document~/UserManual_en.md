## 【Overview】
---
AleToonURP is an `NPR` (anime / toon) rendering shader for Unity URP.\
This user manual explains the `features` and `usage` of every function in the shader's custom GUI, following the order of the foldout panels, to help you quickly understand and master how to tune each rendering feature.\
It is recommended to adjust the material parameters and observe the results in real time as you read.
